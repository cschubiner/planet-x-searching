Tutorial: How to Play

Welcome to Planet X Searching!

This is a companion app for the board game "The Search for Planet X".

Important: You still need the official iOS/Android app to play the game. This web app helps you track your notes, deductions, and game state.
What You'll Learn:
  • The goal of the game
  • Available actions and their time costs
  • How to use this companion app
  • Tips for tracking your deductions

Game Overview

You are an astronomer searching for the mysterious Planet X. The sky is divided into sectors containing various objects:

Planet X
Asteroid

Key Rule: Planet X always appears empty when you survey or target its sector!

The Time Track

Every action costs time . The player furthest behind always goes next.

Action Time Cost What It Does
Survey 2-4 Learn how many of an object type are in a range of sectors
Target 4 Learn exactly what's in one sector (limited: 2 per game)
Research 1 Learn a logic rule about object placement
Locate Planet X 5 Attempt to find Planet X and end the game!
Tip: You cannot Research twice in a row!

Object Placement Rules

Each object follows specific rules that help you deduce locations:

Comets Only in prime sectors (2, 3, 5, 7, 11...)
Asteroids Always adjacent to at least 1 other asteroid
Dwarf Planets Never adjacent to Planet X
Gas Clouds Always adjacent to at least 1 Truly Empty sector
Planet X Never adjacent to Dwarf Planet; appears empty

Using the Hints Table

The Hints Table is your main deduction tool. Track what you learn about each sector:

Green Check

Mark when you confirm an object is in a sector.

Red X

Mark when you rule out an object from a sector.

Auto-helper: When you confirm one object in a sector, the app automatically marks all other objects as "No" for that sector!

Tracking Moves

The Move Notes section helps you track each player's actions:

  1. Select the player taking the action (by color)
  2. Choose the action (Survey, Target, Research, Locate)
  3. Fill in the details (which sectors, which object, etc.)
  4. Add notes about what you learned
The Time Track display shows each player's current position. The player with the lowest time goes next!

Solo Mode: Playing with a Bot

Want to play alone? Solo mode lets you play against an AI bot controlled by the official app!

Key Point: Solo mode is set up as a 2-player game where the bot is the second player.
Setting Up Solo Mode:
  1. Choose TWO player colors in this app - one for you, one for the bot
  2. In the official app: Select "Solo Game" mode (usually at the bottom of the screen)
  3. Give the bot:
    • All 12 theory tokens from the bot's color
    • 12 additional tokens (face up) to verify the bot's choices
Playing Solo Mode:
On Your Turn:

Play normally - choose your action in the official app, record the results in this companion app.

On the Bot's Turn:

The official app will tell you what the bot does! The app automatically:

  • Chooses the bot's action (Survey, Target, Research, etc.)
  • Shows you the details (which sectors, which object type, etc.)
  • Submits theories (only when the bot knows it's correct!)
Recording Bot Actions:

In this companion app, track the bot's moves exactly like a regular player:

  • Select the bot's player color
  • Record the action the app tells you the bot took
  • Add any notes about what the bot might have learned
Winning Solo Mode: Try to locate Planet X before the bot does! The bot is smart and only submits theories it knows are correct.
Pro Tip: Pay attention to when the bot submits theories - it means the bot has deduced that location with certainty!

Theories & Scoring

Submit theories about object locations to score points!

Points per correct theory:
  • Asteroid: 2 points
  • Comet: 3 points
  • Gas Cloud: 4 points
  • Dwarf Planet: 4 points (Standard) / 2 points (Expert)
  • First to Locate Planet X: 10 points

Use the Theory Tracking section to record your submitted theories!

Research & Conferences

Research Topics

There are 6 research topics (A-F). Each reveals a logic rule about 1-2 object types. Track which topics you've researched in the Research Notes section.

Conferences

When players reach certain time thresholds, conferences are triggered:

  • Standard Mode: X1 at time 12
  • Expert Mode: X1 at time 10, X2 at time 22

Conferences reveal information about Planet X's relationship to nearby objects!

Ready to Play!

Quick Start Guide:
  1. Choose your mode (Standard: 12 sectors, Expert: 18 sectors)
  2. Set up players by dragging color badges to player slots
  3. Select difficulty (determines starting hints)
  4. Click "Start Game"
  5. Use the official app to perform actions and get results
  6. Record everything in this companion app!
Auto-Save: Your game is automatically saved. If you close the browser, you can resume where you left off!

Good luck finding Planet X!
